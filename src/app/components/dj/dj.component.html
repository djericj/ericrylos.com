DJ

Eric's career as a DJ began all the way back in 1995, by trading in a set of drums for a Technics SL1200 turntable. Since then, Eric has had numerous gigs in clubs and raves with high profile headliners such as Tiesto, Sasha, Sandra Collins, Eric Davenport, Uber Zone, and more. More of a studio rat these days, he can still be heard regularly online as part of the Soundscapes Digital radio show on Saturo Sounds and Proton Radio.

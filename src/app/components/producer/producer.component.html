Producer

Collaborators

Mario P.

Mario has collaborated with Eric for 30 years. Starting with a chance meeting in school, thw two have been working together on musical projects ever since. The two started aas DJ partners and eventually transitioned into forming the electronic duo Mario & Eric J. The Duo prodcued Progressive and Trance music for nearly 15 years before switching to a more laid back, house-influenced sound. The reimagined duo adopted the name Downgroovesm under which they still release music today.

Chris Sterio

Chris has collaborated with both Mario & Eric J for nearly 20 years. After signing the duo's first release to his label Source of Gravity. The pair released 2 E.P.s and a compilation contribution on Source of Gravity Reords and the sub-label Gravitation. Soon, Source of Gravity and Gravitation merged and were reborn under a new label, Soundscaps Digital, specializing the digital only releases. The label continues to release music today, and has release tracks from all aliases, Evotone, Downgrooves, Mario & Eric J and Eric Rylos.

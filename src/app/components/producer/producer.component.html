Producer

Since 1995, Eric has produced numerous original tracks across a wide variety of genres. Eric has also had long time collaborations with DJ's Mario & Chris Sterio, as well as a long relationship with the Soundscapes Digital record label.

Along with his celebrated original works, Eric has remixed tracks for numerous artists and record labels, both inside and outside the traditional genre boundaries of electronic music.


Collaborators

Mario P.

Mario has collaborated with Eric for 30 years. Starting with a chance meeting in school, the two have been working together on musical projects ever since. The two started aas DJ partners and eventually transitioned into forming the electronic duo Mario & Eric J. The Duo prodcued Progressive and Trance music for nearly 15 years before switching to a more laid back, house-influenced sound. The reimagined duo adopted the name Downgrooves, under which they still release music today.

Chris Sterio

Chris has collaborated with both Mario & Eric J for nearly 20 years. After signing the duo's first release to his label Source of Gravity. The pair released 2 E.P.s and a compilation contribution on Source of Gravity Reords and the sub-label Gravitation. Soon, Source of Gravity and Gravitation merged and were reborn under a new label, Soundscaps Digital, specializing the digital only releases. The label continues to put out music today, and has released tracks from all aliases.
